About Artemis

Company Background

Artemis was founded in 2020, during the height of the pandemic, as a creative outlet for our co-founders. What began as a way to stay grounded and inspired during quarantine quickly evolved into a bold fashion label built on cultural storytelling and self-expression.

The brand began in a cozy Mississauga home, where siblings Ayo and Precious stitched early prototypes by hand using limited materials and boundless imagination. Driven by purpose and passion, they transformed their living room into a design lab and their local community into their first runway.

As word spread, so did their mission — to create clothing that empowered people not just to dress well, but to feel seen and celebrated. Today, Artemis stands as a symbol of resilience, heritage, and innovation, blending African influence with European elegance in every collection.

Our Mission

Our mission is to craft clothing that empowers individuals to feel bold, confident, and connected to their culture. We believe fashion is not just about trends — it’s about truth. It’s about owning your story, expressing your identity, and wearing your values.

At Artemis, we aim to make high-quality, expressive fashion accessible to all. We champion creativity, inclusivity, and sustainability in everything we do — from our design sketches to our production process. We’re here to build more than a brand. We’re here to build a movement — one outfit at a time.

Our Values

  1. Creativity – We push boundaries and celebrate originality in every stitch, pattern, and silhouette.
  2. Community – We uplift voices and create fashion that brings people together, locally and globally.
  3. Quality – We value craftsmanship, sourcing high-quality materials and ensuring every piece meets our standards.
  4. Sustainability – We are committed to mindful production, conscious materials, and designs that last beyond trends.
